

Nexon Co., Ltd. is a South Korean video game publisher known for publishing popular titles such as MapleStory, Dungeon & Fighter, Sudden Attack, and KartRider. The company was founded in Seoul, South Korea, in 1994 by Kim Jung-ju and Jake Song. In 2005, Nexon moved its headquarters to Tokyo, Japan. Nexon has offices in South Korea, the United States, Taiwan, and Thailand, and its largest shareholder is the investment firm NXC, which was also founded by Kim Jung-ju.
